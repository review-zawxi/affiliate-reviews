What is Profit Vault (ClickBank Profit Club)?

Profit Vault — officially called ClickBank Profit Club — is a done-for-you affiliate marketing system. The premise: instead of spending months building funnels and writing emails from scratch, you plug into John Thornhill's existing infrastructure and promote it as your own affiliate offer.

According to the official site at cbprofitclub.com, members receive:

  • A complete marketing funnel branded to you and fully automated
  • Pre-written follow-up email sequences ready to deploy
  • Live bi-weekly coaching calls with John Thornhill's team
  • At the VIP level: 12 months of done-for-you member recruitment on a rotational basis

The program is currently in its Founder Phase, meaning pricing is lower than the planned public launch price.

Common questions

Is Profit Vault the same as ClickBank Profit Club?
Yes. Profit Vault is a promotional name. The official product is ClickBank Profit Club at cbprofitclub.com.
Do I need experience to join?
No prior experience required. The system is designed as beginner-friendly with done-for-you assets and bi-weekly coaching.
Is AWeber really mandatory?
Yes. The email funnel is hardwired to AWeber. GetResponse, ConvertKit, and other providers are not compatible with the done-for-you setup.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. A 14-day money-back guarantee is offered through ClickBank's standard refund process, which is reliable and well-established.
How much can I earn?
Results vary based on your traffic and marketing consistency. The official site clearly states income is not guaranteed and depends on individual effort.

Final verdict
